# TFK — Social Media Guidelines

*Working playbook for TFK's social channels, aligned with the Brand Guidelines. July 2026.*

## Channels

| Channel | Handle | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | @instatfk | Primary brand channel — image, campaigns, community |
| TikTok | @tfk.store | Discovery, younger audience, scent storytelling |
| YouTube | @TheFragranceKitchen | Long-form brand films and campaign content |
| WhatsApp | +965 6363 0075 | Concierge commerce & customer service |

## Content Pillars (recommended mix)

1. **Scent Stories (30%)** — the fragrance as hero: notes, mood, the memory it evokes. Poetic captions built from the product descriptors ("Saffron rose with royal allure").
2. **Heritage & House (20%)** — founder story, Kuwaiti heritage, bukhoor ritual, craftsmanship, the store experience (the smoke-wall flagship is highly photogenic).
3. **The Fragrance Journey (20%)** — education and discovery: how to build a scent wardrobe, layering with mists, Discovery Set unboxings, "which TFK are you?" formats.
4. **Occasions & Gifting (20%)** — Ramadan/Eid, National Day, weddings, graduations; curated sets and gift cards as the answer to "what do I gift?"
5. **Community & Social Proof (10%)** — reviews, UGC, fragrance-community reactions, store visits.

## Tone per Platform

- **Instagram:** Editorial elegance. Short, evocative captions; Arabic + English where possible. Reels for launches and store atmosphere; carousels for scent-note education.
- **TikTok:** Charming and human, still premium. Talk-to-camera scent reviews, "scent of the day," behind-the-counter content. Wit yes, memes-at-the-brand's-expense no.
- **YouTube:** Cinematic. Brand films, campaign hero videos, founder narrative.
- **WhatsApp:** Personal concierge. Greet by name, recommend by occasion and taste, confirm orders graciously.

## Visual Rules

- Bottles are heroes: clean backgrounds, warm luxury lighting, consistent with website photography.
- Product names appear in ALL CAPS, matching packaging and site.
- Logo per brand guidelines (black on light / white on dark); never stretched, recolored, or overlaid on busy imagery.
- Prices in social captions only for entry products (Discovery Sets, Mists); keep Signature/Exclusive tiers story-led, price on site.

## Posting & Campaign Rhythm (starting framework)

- Instagram: 4–5 feed posts/week + daily stories during campaigns
- TikTok: 3–4 videos/week
- YouTube: aligned to launches/campaigns
- Key calendar anchors: Ramadan & Eid Al-Fitr, Eid Al-Adha, Kuwait National & Liberation Days (Feb 25–26), Mother's Day (March 21 in the Gulf), summer travel, back-to-school/graduation, White Friday/year-end gifting.

## Measurement (ties into this project's performance reviews)

Track monthly, and reconcile with Shopify referral analytics (`order_referrer_source = social`):
- Reach, engagement rate, follower growth per channel
- Click-throughs to tfk.com.kw and social-attributed sessions
- Social-attributed orders, revenue, and conversion rate (via Shopify sessions/sales by referrer)
- Best-performing content pillar per month → feed learnings into next month's mix

## Guardrails

- No engagement-bait, no aggressive discounting language, no dupe comparisons.
- Respect cultural and religious sensitivities in all markets (Kuwait, UAE), especially during Ramadan (adjust tone, timing, and imagery).
- Influencer/UGC partnerships must match luxury positioning; always disclose paid partnerships per platform rules.
- Customer complaints in comments → acknowledge gracefully in public, resolve in private (DM/WhatsApp).
